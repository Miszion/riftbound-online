## Findings

- **GraphQL `matchReplay` query works end-to-end.** All 10 self-play replays return non-null `finalState` plus a non-empty `moves` array.
- **`finalState` schema** includes: `matchId`, `status`, `winner`, `turnNumber`, `currentPhase`, `players[]` (with `board.{creatures,artifacts,enchantments}`, `hand`, `graveyard`, `exile`, `channeledRunes`, `runeDeck`, `championLegend`, `championLeader`, `handSize`, `deckCount`, `runeDeckSize`, `victoryPoints`, `victoryScore`, `mana`, `maxMana`, `resources`, `burnedOut`, `temporaryEffects`), `battlefields[]` (with `card`, `hiddenCards`, `presence`, `controller`, `contestedBy`), `moveHistory`, `snapshots`, `scoreLog`, `duelLog`, `chatLog`, `reactionChain`, `priorityWindow`, `pendingSpellResolution`, `prompts`, `combatContext`, `victoryScore`, `endReason`, `initiativeWinner/Loser/SelectedAt`.
- **`assets` field survives serialization.** 98.5% of cards walked in `finalState` have a populated `assets` object with `remote` (https://static.dotgg.gg/riftbound/...) and `localPath`. The small shortfall is battlefield cards whose `CardSnapshot` did not populate `assets` (legitimate — they're location objects, not card copies).
- **Card types observed:** `creature`, `spell`, `artifact`, `battlefield`. NOT observed in this self-play sample: `enchantment`, explicit `rune`-typed card (runes appear as tile objects under `channeledRunes` with `runeId`/`domain`/`energyValue` shape, not as `type: "rune"` card objects), token/summoned cards, `legend`/`leader` champion objects (self-play decks use `championLegend: null`, `championLeader: null`).
- **Hidden-card flow not exercised by self-play.** Zero `hide_card` / `activate_hidden` moves and zero `hiddenCards[]` entries across all 10 replays. The reducer branches at `reducer.ts:213,227` cannot be validated without a replay that exercises them — flag this for a scripted test fixture or a human play session.
- **Ability activation not exercised.** Zero `activate_ability` moves. Same caveat.
- **Action-name mismatch vs QA doc.** The QA doc lists `move` but the engine emits `move_unit` (16 total across 10 replays). Either the doc should be updated or the reducer should alias both.
- **No crashes or stack traces in the backend log during any of the 10 self-play matches** beyond the expected bot `dispatch error` messages (bots probing invalid actions — these are soft errors, not crashes).
